# CashKey Property Group - Website Interaction Design

## Core User Journey & Lead Generation Flow

### Primary Goal: Convert visitors into qualified leads for cash home purchases

**Target Audience**: Homeowners looking to sell quickly for cash, including those with:
- Distressed properties
- Inherited homes
- Financial difficulties
- Relocation needs
- Properties needing extensive repairs

## Interactive Components

### 1. Lead Capture Form System
**Location**: Prominently on homepage, dedicated "Get Offer" page
**Functionality**: 
- Multi-step form with progressive disclosure
- Step 1: Property address and basic contact info
- Step 2: Property condition, reason for selling, timeline
- Step 3: Additional details, preferred contact method
- Real-time validation and progress indicator
- Immediate confirmation message with next steps

### 2. Property Evaluation Calculator
**Location**: Homepage, services page
**Functionality**:
- Interactive tool where users input property details
- Slider inputs for square footage, bedrooms, bathrooms
- Condition assessment with visual icons
- Estimated cash offer range display
- Call-to-action to submit for exact offer

### 3. Success Stories Carousel
**Location**: Homepage, testimonials section
**Functionality**:
- Auto-rotating testimonials from satisfied sellers
- Before/after property images
- Customer photos and quotes
- Interactive navigation dots and arrows
- Filter by property type or situation

### 4. Service Area Map
**Location**: Contact page, footer
**Functionality**:
- Interactive map showing service areas
- Clickable regions with coverage details
- "We Buy In Your Area" confirmation
- Quick form for area verification

## User Interaction Flow

### Homepage Journey:
1. **Hero Section**: Immediate value proposition with prominent "Get Cash Offer" button
2. **Credibility Indicators**: Company stats, BBB rating, customer count
3. **Process Explanation**: 3-step visual process (Contact → Evaluate → Close)
4. **Lead Capture**: Embedded quick form or prominent CTA to full form
5. **Social Proof**: Testimonials and recent purchases
6. **FAQ Section**: Address common concerns and objections

### Lead Generation Strategy:
- Multiple conversion points throughout site
- Exit-intent popup with special offer
- Live chat integration for immediate assistance
- Click-to-call functionality for mobile users
- Downloadable guides in exchange for contact info

### Mobile Optimization:
- Click-to-call buttons prominently displayed
- Simplified forms with minimal fields
- Thumb-friendly button sizes
- Quick contact options always visible

## Trust Building Elements:
- Company credentials and licenses
- Customer testimonials with photos
- Recent purchase examples
- Clear process transparency
- No-obligation guarantee messaging
- Professional photography and design

## Call-to-Action Hierarchy:
1. Primary: "Get Cash Offer" (main conversion)
2. Secondary: "Learn How It Works" (education)
3. Tertiary: "See Success Stories" (social proof)

This interaction design focuses on building trust while making it incredibly easy for potential sellers to request a cash offer and begin the sales process.
